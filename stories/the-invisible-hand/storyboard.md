# The Invisible Hand

> *Mission: Knowledge Story — Supply, Demand, and Market Equilibrium*
> *Location: Medieval Village Marketplace*
> *Domain: science*

## Sequence Overview

The story of how competition and self-interest naturally drive markets toward equilibrium. Through a village bakery parable, teaches supply and demand, price competition, and Adam Smith's concept of the invisible hand guiding decentralized markets toward efficient outcomes.
